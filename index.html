Password Cracking Demo

Educational demonstrations of password attack techniques

Welcome!

This site contains browser-based demonstrations of common password cracking techniques. These demos are designed for educational purposes to help understand the importance of strong passwords and secure authentication practices.

Available Demonstrations

Brute Force Attack

Available

A brute force attack systematically tries every possible combination of characters until the correct password is found. This demo shows how computational power can crack simple passwords.

Dictionary Attack

Available

A dictionary attack uses a pre-compiled list of common words and passwords to attempt to crack a password. This is often faster and more efficient than brute force for common passwords.

Credential Stuffing

Available

Simulates reusing leaked credentials across multiple sites to show how password reuse can grant attackers quick access to many accounts.
